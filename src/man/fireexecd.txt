.SH NAME
Fireexecd \- Daemon that assists sandboxed processes to execute protected apps or open protected files.
.SH SYNOPSIS
fireexecd [OPTIONS]
.SH DESCRIPTION
Fireexecd assists sandboxed processes to execute protected apps or open protected files.
.SH OPTIONS
.TP
\fB\-?\fR, \fB\-\-help\fR
Print options end exit.
.TP
\fB\-\-version
Print program version and exit.
